Explore Diverse Eras

Anachrone offers a unique opportunity to explore some of the most iconic periods in history, as well as futuristic worlds beyond imagination. Thanks to our advanced neural VR system, each era feels as real as the present day. Take control of your character’s actions with your thoughts and dive into thrilling adventures across the following time periods:

Ancient Civilizations

Walk among the temples of Egypt, Greece, and Rome. Experience life as a gladiator, philosopher, or explorer as you uncover the secrets of these long-lost worlds.

Medieval Europe

Enter the heart of medieval society, where knights, kings, and courtly intrigue await. Test your mettle in jousting tournaments, command armies in epic battles, or influence the future of a kingdom through diplomacy and wit.

Golden Age of Piracy

Sail the high seas with a crew of fearsome pirates. Plan daring raids, hunt for treasure, or outwit rival captains as you experience the rough-and-tumble life of a pirate.

Futuristic Worlds

Step into the future, where cities float in the sky, interstellar travel is common, and artificial intelligence rules. Will you rise as a leader in these high-tech utopias, or struggle for survival in dystopian landscapes?

Each time period offers its own unique challenges, quests, and characters, with evolving storylines that ensure every visit is different. The power to shape history—and your destiny—lies within your thoughts.
